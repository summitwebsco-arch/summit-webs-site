06 FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a new website?

Most Starter and Pro websites are completed within 1-2 weeks after we finalize your content and design direction.

I already have a website — can you still help?

Absolutely. Many of our clients come to us with an outdated or underperforming website. We'll review what you have, show you a free concept for an upgrade, and handle the full transition.

What happens if I need changes after my site is live?

That's exactly what our monthly care plans are for. Plans start at $50/mo and include hosting, security, and support — higher tiers add edits, SEO content, and managed ads.

Do I own my website?

Yes — the website is built for your business. Our monthly plan covers hosting, management, and ongoing improvements, similar to how you might pay for software or tools you rely on.

What's included in the monthly care plan?

Hosting, security updates, uptime monitoring, monthly performance reports, and regular content edits — plus optional add-ons like local SEO blog content and managed ad campaigns.

Do you only work with landscaping companies?

Landscaping and lawn care is our primary focus right now, which means we deeply understand what helps these businesses get more calls and bookings online — but we're happy to talk if you're in a related trade.
