Quick Answers

Do I actually see the design before I pay?
Yes — always. We build a full mockup of your homepage (sometimes the full site) before you pay a single dollar. You review it, we revise if needed, and only once you're happy do we send an invoice and go live.
What if I don't like what you build?
Tell us and we'll revise it. Our mockup process includes two rounds of changes. If after revisions it's still not a fit, you walk away — no charge, no awkwardness. We've never had that happen, but the option is there.
Can I cancel the monthly plan?
Yes, any time. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Just let us know 30 days before your next billing date and we'll wrap things up — including handing over your files if you want to take it somewhere else.
What does the monthly plan actually include?
At minimum (Essential): hosting, SSL certificate, security monitoring, and weekly backups — everything your site needs to stay online and secure. Growth adds monthly SEO updates, content tweaks, and a monthly Google Analytics summary. Pro adds managed Google Ads and unlimited edit requests.
Do you only work with landscaping companies?
Yes — we specialize exclusively in landscaping, lawn care, tree service, and snow removal companies in Northeast Ohio. That means every template, every line of copy, and every SEO strategy we use is tuned specifically for your industry and your local market.
