06 FAQ

Questions We Get a Lot

How long until my site is live?

For most Basic and Pro sites, we're live within 1–2 weeks of you giving the go-ahead. Premium sites with more pages usually take closer to two weeks.

I already have a website — can you still help?

Yeah, absolutely. We'll take a look at what you've got, put together a free concept for an upgrade, and handle the whole switchover if you decide to move forward.

What if I need changes after my site is live?

That's what the monthly plan covers. Starting at $50/month you get hosting, security, and edits. Need SEO blog posts or Google Ads? Higher plans have that covered.

Do I own my website?

Yes, it's yours. The monthly fee covers hosting and ongoing management, like paying for any tool you use every day.

What's actually in the monthly care plan?

At minimum: hosting, security monitoring, automatic backups, and at least one edit per month. Higher plans add more edits, a monthly SEO blog post we write for you, and managed Google Ads.

Do you only work with landscaping companies?

Right now, yes. And that's intentional. We know this industry, we know what homeowners in NE Ohio search for, and we've built everything around it.
