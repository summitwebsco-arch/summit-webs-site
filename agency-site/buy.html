Quick Answers
Do I actually see the design before I pay? +
Yes — always. We build a full homepage mockup before you pay a single dollar. You review it, we revise if needed, and only once you're happy do we send an invoice and go live.
What if I don't like what you build? +
Tell us and we'll revise it. Our mockup process includes two rounds of changes. If after revisions it's still not a fit, you walk away — no charge, no awkwardness.
Can I cancel the monthly plan? +
Yes, any time. No contracts or cancellation fees. Just let us know 30 days before your next billing date and we'll wrap things up — including handing over your files if you want them.
What does the monthly care plan actually include? +
Essential ($50/mo): hosting, SSL, security monitoring, automatic backups, and 1 edit/month. Growth ($100/mo) adds 4 edits, an SEO blog post, and a monthly analytics report. Pro ($150/mo) adds managed Google Ads, unlimited edits, and priority support.
Do you only work with landscaping companies? +
Yes — we specialize in landscaping, lawn care, tree service, and snow removal companies in Northeast Ohio. Every template, every line of copy, and every SEO strategy is tuned for your industry and local market.
